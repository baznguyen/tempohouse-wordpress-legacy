## Why Editorial Placement Matters

TEMPO House's current English editorial footprint is near-zero. Manzi Art Space (Hanoi) — the closest format equivalent in Vietnam — appears in TripAdvisor, AFAR, Vietnam Tourism, Rusty Compass, and multiple travel blogs. The category (art gallery café) has a dominant player in Hanoi and zero in HCMC. The first venue to claim this position in English press earns a durable SEO moat — links from authoritative domains compound over time.

**The gap is not conceptual. It's executional. Nobody has pitched these outlets yet.**

## Pitch Strategy by Outlet Type

### Vietnam Coracle
Tom Hall writes honest, firsthand reviews. He cannot be pitched with a press release. Strategy:
- Host a private evening at no cost
- Invite him as a guest, not as press
- Let him discover it; if he loves it, he writes about it
- Timeline: 3-6 months from GMB/TripAdvisor being live (he cross-checks)

### Vietcetera / Saigoneer
These accept contributed features and have editorial relationships:
- Pitch a byline piece or interview format
- Angle: the "art changes" story — the rotating gallery, what it means to the Saigon arts ecosystem
- Include: high-quality photography (essential; both outlets are visual)
- Timeline: Can pitch within 30 days of having GMB + photography assets ready

### Vietnamese Social / TikTok
Not editorial — creator relationships:
- Identify 5-10 micro-influencers (10k-100k, based in D1-D3)
- Host a creative-evening media event (invite creator community, not press)
- Brief them on the day-to-night transformation (most visual, most shareable)
- Cost: drinks + atmosphere; no cash required at this stage

## Content Hooks (What Editors Actually Want)

These are the angles that will get TEMPO placed. Use these in pitches:

1. **"The Art Changes"** — rotating exhibition model, the gallery never stays the same. Strong visual story for photo-led outlets.

2. **"Pasteur Street D3 — What the Travel Guides Don't Know"** — neighbourhood angle, D3 invisible in English content. Saigoneer's bread and butter.

3. **"Same Address, Three Moods"** — the day/evening/night transformation. TikTok viral concept, works in long-form too.

4. **"The Saigon Manzi"** — explicitly frame TEMPO as Saigon's answer to Manzi Hanoi for any editor who knows the Hanoi scene. Vietcetera / Asia Bars.

5. **"Craft Cocktails, Zero Velvet Rope"** — the anti-exclusivity bar story. Asia Bars & Restaurants, The Dot Magazine.

6. **"Where the Reunification Palace Meets Art and Coffee"** — Tourism/AFAR angle. 500m from Saigon's top tourist landmark, nobody has written this.

## Photography Requirements

No editorial outlet will publish without professional photography. This is the blocker before any media outreach is meaningful.

Required assets:
- Hero interior shots (morning light + evening/cocktail light) — the transformation is the story
- Gallery level: individual artwork shots + room shots with guests
- Bar programme: cocktails, craft detail, bartender at work
- Exterior: 218c Pasteur facade (French-colonial architecture)
- Portrait capability for chef/founder/artist interviews
