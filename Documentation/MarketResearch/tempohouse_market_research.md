## The Venue (30-second brief)

**TEMPO House** — 218c Pasteur Street, District 3, Ho Chi Minh City  
Specialty café (07:00–17:00) · Craft cocktail bar (18:00–01:00) · Rotating art gallery (Level 1, always) · Private event hire (full building)  
Capacity: 60 seated café · 80 standing gallery · 150+ full venue  
Founded by Aussie-Vietnamese creatives. Business in early-stage, in debt, performance needs turning around.  
**Core concept:** Different tempos throughout the day — art × gallery × café × cocktail bar × event space × experience space.

**Positioning statement:** "The only place in Saigon where the same room earns your morning focus, your late-night conversation, and the art on the wall — because craft doesn't clock out."

**Critical status (June 2026):** Zero English editorial presence. Not in Vietcetera, Saigoneer, Vietnam Coracle, TripAdvisor, The Dot Magazine, or Asia Bars. Near-zero Vietnamese aggregator presence. Building and concept are strong; discovery and commercial performance are not.
