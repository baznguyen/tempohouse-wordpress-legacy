## 3. Corporate vs Personal Event Search Patterns

### Corporate Event Planners
- **Search behaviour:** More deliberate, longer queries, often include capacity/tech specs ("conference room 50 pax Saigon", "meeting room with AV District 1").
- **Key differentiators they search for:** capacity numbers, AV equipment, catering, parking, proximity to business districts.
- **Primary platforms:** Google Search → hotel websites directly. Also LinkedIn recommendations and word-of-mouth. Aggregators (Venuerific) not active in VN market.
- **Key terms:** "corporate event venue", "meeting venue", "conference space", "year end party venue", "team building venue Saigon".
- **Seasonality:** Strong Q4 spike (Oct–Dec) for year-end parties; Q1 for strategy offsites; Q2 for product launches.
- **District preference:** Corporate searchers default to District 1 and Binh Thanh (luxury hotels). District 3 is under-represented but accessible — a gap Tempo House can exploit with content that positions Pasteur Street as a creative, accessible alternative to the CBD.

### Personal Event Planners
- **Search behaviour:** Shorter, more emotional queries; driven by occasion ("intimate birthday venue Saigon", "where to have a birthday party in HCMC").
- **Key differentiators they search for:** atmosphere/vibe, Instagram-worthiness, private vs semi-private, food/drink packages.
- **Primary platforms:** Google Search, Google Maps, TikTok (very strong in VN), Instagram, Zalo groups, Facebook groups ("Expats in Saigon", "HCMC Events & Nightlife").
- **Key terms:** "birthday party venue Saigon", "private room restaurant Saigon", "baby shower venue", "engagement party venue", "unique venue Saigon".
- **Seasonality:** Personal events spread year-round; birthday spikes follow calendar. Tet (Jan/Feb) = low event hire season; Western holiday period (Dec) = high.

### Key Insight on Platform Landscape
Timeout HCMC's venue content is completely dead (all 404). TripAdvisor blocks crawlers but ranks well. There is **no dominant aggregator for HCMC event venues** equivalent to Peerspace (US) or Venuerific (SG). This is a structural gap: the market is fragmented between hotel pages and individual venue sites, with no curated booking platform. Google Maps is therefore the most powerful discovery channel for boutique venues.

## 6. SEO Tactical Recommendations for Tempo House

### Google Business Profile (Highest ROI, Fastest Win)
- Ensure GBP is verified at 218c Pasteur St, Quan 3
- Primary category: "Event Venue" — add secondaries: "Party Venue", "Wedding Venue", "Meeting Room"
- Upload 20+ photos: space configurations, lighting moods, capacity setups, food/drinks
- Collect reviews mentioning specific event types (birthday, corporate, photoshoot)
- Post weekly about upcoming events/availability
- This alone captures the map pack for "event venue District 3" and "event space near me"

### Landing Page Architecture
Create dedicated pages targeting each keyword cluster:
- `/events/corporate-events/` — targets B2B corporate terms
- `/events/birthday-parties/` — targets personal celebration searches
- `/events/weddings/` — even if niche (intimate/elopement), worth targeting
- `/events/creative-hire/` — photo shoots, film locations, creative productions
- `/events/private-dining/` — dinner party, engagement, anniversary
- Each page needs: capacity info, pricing indication, photo gallery, inquiry CTA

### Content Gaps to Fill (Timeout Left the Building)
Timeout HCMC's venue content is all 404. Write a "Best Event Venues in Ho Chi Minh City" style post on the Tempo House blog that:
- Ranks for discovery searches
- Includes Tempo House + 4–5 complementary (non-competing) venues
